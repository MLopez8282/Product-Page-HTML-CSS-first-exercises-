Bonsai

$19.99

The purposes of bonsai are primarily contemplation for the
viewer, and the pleasant exercise of effort and ingenuity for
the grower.

By contrast with other plant cultivation practices, bonsai is
not intended for production of food or medicine. Instead,
bonsai practice focuses on long-term cultivation and shaping
of one or more small trees growing in a container.
